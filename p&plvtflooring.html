P&P LVT Flooring

Luxury Vinyl Tile is a floor that looks like real wood or stone but is actually made up of a series of layers, flexible and thin enough to cut through with a knife. It is a durable product that is a great way to add warmth to your home, like carpet, but with the practicalities of a tile. Luxury vinyl flooring is good option for those on a tighter budget as you can install it yourself. it is perfect for your homes, offices. Easy to clean and maintain.
